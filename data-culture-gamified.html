✅ What Should Be Captured — And Where
TouchpointIdeal SystemData CapturedI Do This
Subscriber ListGoogle SheetName, phone, address, plan, start date, dietary needs
Daily Delivery LogWhatsApp checklist / sheetWho got tiffin, who cancelled, time of delivery
Cost Per MealWeekly ingredient logIngredient cost ÷ meals delivered = actual margin per tiffin
Cancellation LogCancellation registerWho cancelled, when, reason — to spot patterns and reduce waste

📉 What Actually Happens
  • Subscriber list maintained in memory or a rough WhatsApp group
  • Cancellations received via WhatsApp — never logged, vegetables bought in excess anyway
  • No cost per meal ever calculated — pricing set by guessing market rates
  • Payment tracked by counting bank credits and hoping they match subscribers
  • Churn happens silently — someone stops ordering and it's noticed only after 2 weeks
  • Seasonal demand not predicted — raw materials over/under-purchased around festivals
💸 What This Gap Costs You
No Cost Per Meal Tracking
You charge ₹120 per meal. Actual ingredient cost is ₹95 on some days. Your margin is ₹25. After gas, packaging, and delivery — you're working for almost nothing.
Cancellation Waste
5 cancellations per day, vegetables purchased for full count. That's ₹50–80 wasted daily. In a month: ₹1,500–2,400 in vegetable wastage alone.
No Churn Detection
You had 40 subscribers in January. Now you have 32. 8 people left. You don't know when, why, or who. A simple goodbye message + reason-ask could have retained 3–4 of them.
Unpredictable Cash Flow
3 subscribers haven't paid in 6 weeks. ₹2,160 outstanding. You find out when you're short on cash for this week's grocery run.
