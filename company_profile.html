Gaming Industry — Full Visibility in 90 Days

Gaming

A leading gaming company struggled with fragmented data across sales, player behaviour and in-game metrics — making it impossible to present credible numbers to investors.

The Challenge
  • Siloed sales & revenue reports
  • No real-time player metrics
  • Inability to track game health
  • Weak investor reporting
Our Solution
  • Unified dashboard — sales, revenue & post-purchase cohort matrix in real time
  • In-game metrics: DAU, MAU, avg session time, avg games played, login trends
  • Retention tracking: M3 / M6 / M9 cohorts
  • In-app purchase & VIP user analysis
  • Game difficulty level & seasonality analysis
Business Impact
  • Full data consolidated in one dashboard within 3 months
  • Company could verify game health at a glance
  • Real facts pushed to investors — enabled higher growth funding
  • Seasonality insights unlocked precision marketing

Business Impact
  • High profitability restored at the lot level
